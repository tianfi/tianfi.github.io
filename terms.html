1. About DoseCue

DoseCue is an iOS health reminder tool designed to help users create medication, supplement, injection, care, or wellness-related reminder plans. The App helps users:

  • create reminder plans;
  • record whether a task was completed or missed;
  • confirm actual completion times;
  • adjust future reminder times based on user-confirmed events;
  • organize plans by person, pet, or other optional ownership labels.

DoseCue is designed as a local-first tool. Core reminder plans and records are intended to be stored on your device by default. The App does not require a custom account system or our own business server to provide its core reminder features.
