1. About DoseCue

DoseCue is an iOS health reminder app designed to help users create medication, supplement, injection, care, or wellness-related reminder plans. The App helps users:

  • create reminder plans;
  • record whether a task was completed, missed, or skipped;
  • confirm actual completion times;
  • adjust future reminder times based on user-confirmed events;
  • organize plans by person, family member, child, pet, or another optional ownership label;
  • view history, progress, and reminder-related information.

DoseCue is designed around a local-first experience. The App does not require our own account system or developer-operated business server for its core reminder functionality.

DoseCue does not currently provide a calendar integration feature. If calendar-related features are added in a future version, these terms or the Privacy Policy may be updated to describe them.

DoseCue does not require a custom account system, and we do not operate our own business server for the App's core reminder functionality.
