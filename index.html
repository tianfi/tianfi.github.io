DoseCue

A simple reminder and record-keeping app for medication, supplements, and care routines.

DoseCue helps you create reminder plans, confirm actual completion times, record missed tasks, and adjust upcoming reminders based on your real actions.

Unlike a fixed alarm, DoseCue is designed around dynamic scheduling. When you confirm a task, the next reminder can be calculated from the time you actually completed it.

Designed for everyday use

DoseCue can help organize reminders for yourself, a family member, a child, or a pet. Your core app data is designed to stay local-first, with optional Apple platform features such as iCloud sync and iOS widgets.
