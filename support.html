关于 DoseCue

DoseCue 是一款智能服药提醒应用，专为需要长期服药的人群设计。它不仅提供准时提醒，更重要的是支持动态顺延——当您的服药时间因通勤、加班或临时安排而变化时，后续提醒会根据您确认的实际时间进行调整。

核心功能

  • 智能服药提醒，支持本地通知和闹钟提醒（iOS 26+）
  • 动态顺延：根据您确认的服药时间调整后续提醒
  • 多对象管理：为家人、宠物分别设置服药计划
  • 详细的服药历史记录和统计分析
  • 剩余药量追踪和补药提醒
  • 跨日处理：智能处理跨 midnight 的服药场景
  • 数据导出：支持 CSV 和 PDF 格式
  • iCloud 同步：跨设备同步数据（Pro 版）
  • 小组件和灵动岛支持

常见问题

Q: 什么是"动态顺延"？

A: 动态顺延是 DoseCue 的核心功能。当您在非计划时间确认服药后，后续的提醒时间会根据您设置的时间进行调整，而不是继续按照原计划时间提醒。这更符合真实生活场景。

Q: 如何处理跨日的服药情况？

A: DoseCue 提供了完善的跨日处理机制。当任务跨越 midnight 时，系统根据您的设置处理并提供"继续昨天"或"结束今天"等选项，让您可以灵活处理。

Q: 免费版和 Pro 版有什么区别？

A: 免费版支持 2 个启用药品和 1 个对象，包含动态顺延和本地通知功能，可查看最近 7 天历史。Pro 版解锁无限药品和对象、完整统计、iCloud 同步、小组件和更强的闹钟提醒。

Q: 如何备份我的数据？

A: 您可以通过设置 > 数据导出功能，将服药记录导出为 CSV 或 PDF 格式进行备份。Pro 版用户还可以使用 iCloud 同步功能备份数据。

Q: 提醒没有响怎么办？

A: 请检查：1) 系统设置中是否允许 DoseCue 发送通知；2) 是否开启了勿扰模式；3) 应用是否在后台运行。iOS 26 及以上版本还可以使用更强的闹钟提醒模式。

About DoseCue

DoseCue is a smart medication reminder app designed for people who need long-term medication. It not only provides timely reminders but also supports dynamic rescheduling — when your dose time changes due to commuting, work, or last-minute plans, subsequent reminders adjust based on your confirmed dose time.

Core Features

  • Smart medication reminders with local notifications and alarm support (iOS 26+)
  • Dynamic rescheduling: adjusts reminders based on your confirmed dose times
  • Multi-owner management: set up medication plans for family members and pets
  • Detailed medication history and statistical analysis
  • Remaining quantity tracking and refill reminders
  • Cross-day handling: intelligently manages dose scenarios crossing midnight
  • Data export: supports CSV and PDF formats
  • iCloud sync: cross-device data synchronization (Pro)
  • Widgets and Live Activity support

FAQ

Q: What is "dynamic rescheduling"?

A: Dynamic rescheduling is DoseCue's core feature. When you confirm a dose at an unscheduled time, subsequent reminder times adjust based on your confirmed time, rather than continuing to follow the original schedule. This better fits real-life scenarios.

Q: How does DoseCue handle cross-day scenarios?

A: DoseCue provides a comprehensive cross-day handling mechanism. When a task crosses midnight, the system processes it based on your settings and provides options like "Continue yesterday" or "End today" for flexible management.

Q: What's the difference between Free and Pro?

A: Free supports 2 active medications and 1 owner, includes dynamic rescheduling and local notifications, and shows the last 7 days of history. Pro unlocks unlimited medications and owners, full stats, iCloud sync, widgets, and stronger alarm reminders.

Q: How do I backup my data?

A: You can export your medication records as CSV or PDF files via Settings > Data Export. Pro users can also use iCloud sync to backup data.

Q: What if reminders don't work?

A: Please check: 1) Whether DoseCue is allowed to send notifications in system settings; 2) Whether Do Not Disturb mode is enabled; 3) Whether the app is running in the background. iOS 26 and later also supports stronger alarm-style reminders.
