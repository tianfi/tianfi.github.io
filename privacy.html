1. About DoseCue

DoseCue is an iOS health reminder tool designed to help users create, confirm, adjust, and record medication, supplement, injection, care, or wellness-related reminder plans.

DoseCue helps users organize reminders and records. It is not a medical service, pharmacy service, veterinary service, emergency service, or clinical decision-making system.

2. Information You Enter in the App

Depending on how you use DoseCue, you may enter or generate information such as:

  • medication names, supplement names, injection names, or care task names;
  • daily reminder counts;
  • reminder intervals;
  • first expected reminder times;
  • next-day reminder preferences;
  • actual completion times confirmed by you;
  • missed or skipped task records;
  • task history and progress information;
  • optional notes;
  • optional ownership labels, such as “Me,” a family member, a child, or a pet;
  • app preferences, such as notification, alarm, reminder, or sync settings.

This information may be health-related or sensitive depending on what you choose to enter. DoseCue is designed to store this information locally on your device by default.

3. Local Storage

DoseCue is designed as a local-first app. Your reminder plans, task projections, completion records, missed records, ownership labels, preferences, notes, and related app data are stored on your device by default.

We do not have access to the local DoseCue data stored on your device unless you choose to share it with us, for example by sending us an email or support message that includes screenshots, exported files, or other information.

If you delete the App, erase your device, disable relevant system storage, or remove app data, your local DoseCue data may be lost unless you have enabled an available backup or sync feature.

4. No Developer-Operated Server Collection

DoseCue does not require a custom account system and does not rely on our own business server for its core reminder functionality.

We do not collect, upload, sell, or share the following information with our own servers:

  • medication names;
  • supplement names;
  • reminder schedules;
  • ownership labels for people, family members, children, or pets;
  • dose completion records;
  • missed or skipped records;
  • cross-day reminder behavior;
  • health-related notes;
  • local app history and progress records;
  • widget content generated from your app data;
  • iCloud-synced DoseCue data.

We do not operate a developer-controlled cloud database for your core DoseCue reminder data.

5. Apple Platform Services

DoseCue uses Apple platform services and system capabilities to provide app functionality. These may include local notifications, alarm-related system capabilities, App Store services, in-app purchases, iCloud, widgets, device storage, and other iOS features.

Apple may process certain information according to your Apple ID, device settings, iCloud settings, App Store settings, payment settings, and Apple's own terms and privacy practices.

We do not receive your full payment card information from Apple.

DoseCue does not currently provide a calendar integration feature. If calendar-related features are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy may be updated to describe how those features work.

6. iCloud Sync

DoseCue offers iCloud sync as part of its supported app functionality. If you enable iCloud sync, your DoseCue app data may be synced through Apple's iCloud services using your Apple ID and device settings.

Synced data may include reminder plans, medication or supplement names, ownership labels, reminder schedules, completion records, missed or skipped records, notes, preferences, and related app data.

We do not operate our own cloud server for DoseCue's core reminder data. Data processed by Apple services is subject to Apple's terms, Apple's privacy policy, and your iCloud settings.

You can manage iCloud and app-related sync settings through iOS Settings and your Apple ID settings.

7. Notifications and Alarm Features

DoseCue may ask for notification or alarm-related permissions so it can remind you about scheduled tasks. Notification content is generated on your device based on the reminder plans you create.

The App may use local notifications, alarm-related system capabilities, sounds, badges, or other Apple-provided reminder features. These features are controlled in part by iOS permissions, device settings, Focus modes, sound settings, notification settings, battery state, time zone settings, and operating system behavior.

Notification times, alarm times, and system-level snooze behavior are reminder-layer features. DoseCue's internal task calculations are based on user-confirmed completion times and user-confirmed actions, not merely on notification or snooze times.

You can manage notification permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

8. Widgets

DoseCue provides iOS widgets to help you view reminder-related information more conveniently. Widget content is generated from app data on your device or from app data made available through Apple platform features such as iCloud sync, depending on your settings.

Widget content may display information such as upcoming reminders, medication or supplement names, ownership labels, task status, completion status, and progress information.

Please be aware that widget content may be visible on your device screen depending on your iOS settings, lock screen settings, home screen layout, StandBy settings, notification settings, and where you place the widget.

You can manage widgets through iOS home screen, lock screen, StandBy, or widget settings where supported by your device and iOS version.

9. In-App Purchases

DoseCue may offer free features and paid Pro features. Pro features may be provided through a one-time in-app purchase using Apple's App Store payment system.

Purchases, refunds, taxes, family sharing, and payment processing are handled by Apple according to Apple's applicable App Store terms and policies. We do not receive your full payment card details.

The App may store purchase entitlement status locally or through Apple-provided purchase APIs in order to unlock paid features.

10. Data Export

DoseCue may allow you to export your records for your own use. Exported files are created only when you choose to export data.

Exported files may contain information shown in the App, such as medication names, supplement names, reminder times, completion records, missed records, ownership labels, notes, statistics, and history information.

Once you export or share a file outside DoseCue, the handling of that file depends on the destination you choose, such as Files, Mail, Messages, AirDrop, iCloud Drive, or another app or service.

Please be careful when exporting or sharing health-related information.

11. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

DoseCue does not use third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or tracking technologies.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your health-related app data with advertisers, analytics providers, or data brokers. We do not use your health-related app data for targeted advertising.

We may view aggregated App Store information provided by Apple, such as downloads, purchases, conversion rates, crash reports, or general app performance information, as made available through Apple's developer tools. This information is provided by Apple and is not created by uploading your DoseCue medication plans, dose records, widget content, or iCloud data to our own server.

12. Contact and Support Emails

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to include in your message, such as questions, screenshots, device information, exported files, or other details.

We use this information only to respond to your request, provide support, troubleshoot issues, improve the App, or comply with applicable legal obligations.

Please avoid sending sensitive health information by email unless you understand and accept the risks of doing so.

13. Children, Family Members, and Pets

DoseCue is not intended for unsupervised use by young children. The App may allow users to organize reminders using optional ownership labels, such as a family member, child, or pet.

If you use DoseCue to manage reminders for a child, family member, pet, or dependent, you are responsible for entering accurate information and using the App under appropriate professional guidance.

DoseCue does not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own servers.

14. Data Retention and Deletion

Because DoseCue is designed to store core app data locally on your device by default, your local app data generally remains on your device until you delete it, modify it, export it, disable relevant features, or delete the App.

You may delete or edit plans and records inside the App where supported. You may also delete the App from your device to remove locally stored app data, subject to iOS behavior and any enabled backup or sync settings.

If iCloud sync or other Apple platform storage features are enabled, you may also need to manage or delete related data through iOS Settings, iCloud settings, or your Apple ID settings.

Emails you send to us may be retained as needed to provide support, maintain business records, improve the App, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations.

15. Security

We design DoseCue to minimize developer-operated data collection by keeping core reminder data on your device by default and by using Apple platform services for supported system features such as iCloud sync, notifications, widgets, and in-app purchases.

However, no device, app, operating system, storage method, sync service, or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your device, Apple ID, passcode, biometric settings, backups, iCloud account, and exported files secure.

16. International Users

DoseCue may be available to users in different countries or regions. If you use Apple platform services, contact us by email, make purchases, enable iCloud, use widgets, or export data to another service, information may be processed in accordance with the terms and privacy practices of the relevant service providers and applicable laws.

17. Medical and Health Disclaimer

DoseCue is a reminder and record-keeping tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, medication recommendations, dosage recommendations, drug interaction checks, emergency services, or professional healthcare guidance.

Reminder times, next-task projections, progress indicators, history records, statistics, widgets, and notifications are generated based on information you enter and actions you confirm. They are provided only for personal organization and convenience.

Always consult a qualified doctor, pharmacist, veterinarian, or other appropriate professional regarding medication, supplements, treatment, dosage, timing, missed doses, side effects, or health-related decisions.

If you believe you, another person, or a pet may be experiencing a medical emergency, contact emergency services or a qualified medical or veterinary professional immediately.
